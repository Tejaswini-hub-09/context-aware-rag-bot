# Company FAQ – NovaCart Customer Support

## 1. What is your refund policy?

Customers may request a refund within **30 days** from the date of delivery. To qualify for a refund, the product must be returned in its original condition, unused, and with all accessories and packaging included. Once the returned product is received and inspected, the refund process begins. Approved refunds are credited to the customer's original payment method within **5 to 7 business days**.

If the product is damaged during shipping or a wrong item is delivered, customers are eligible for a full refund or replacement without any additional charges. Refund requests submitted after 30 days are generally not accepted unless required by law or approved under exceptional circumstances.

Gold and Platinum members receive priority refund processing, which usually reduces the refund processing time.

---

## 2. What are your delivery timelines?

Standard delivery typically takes **3 to 7 business days** depending on the customer's location. Orders placed before 2:00 PM are generally processed on the same business day. Orders placed on weekends or public holidays are processed on the next working day.

Express delivery is available in selected cities and usually delivers within **1 to 2 business days**. Delivery times may be extended during festivals, severe weather conditions, or unexpected logistics issues.

Customers receive an email and SMS containing a tracking number once the order has been shipped.

## 4. Who is eligible for premium customer support?

Premium customer support is available **only to Platinum members**.

Platinum members receive:

* Highest priority for all support requests.
* Dedicated support specialists.
* Faster issue resolution whenever possible.
* Priority handling for replacement and refund requests.
* Access to exclusive customer care channels during support hours.

Gold and Silver members receive standard customer support. Gold members may receive priority for certain refund-related requests, but premium customer support remains exclusive to Platinum members.

---

## 5. How can I cancel my account?

Customers may request account cancellation at any time through the Account Settings page or by contacting customer support.

Before the account is permanently deleted:

* All active orders must be completed or cancelled.
* Pending refunds must be processed.
* Store credits and loyalty points may expire.
* Purchase history may no longer be accessible after deletion.

Account cancellation is generally completed within **7 business days** after verification.

Cancelled accounts cannot be recovered once the deletion process is complete.

## 7. How does shipping work?

Shipping charges depend on the order value and delivery location.

Orders above **₹999** qualify for free standard shipping.

Orders below this amount may include a delivery fee based on the destination.

Customers receive shipping updates through email and SMS. If a shipment cannot be delivered because of an incorrect address or repeated failed delivery attempts, the package may be returned to the warehouse. Customers should ensure that shipping information is accurate before placing an order.

---

## 8. What is your return policy?

Products may be returned within **30 days** if they are unused and in their original packaging.

Certain products are not eligible for returns, including:

* Personalized items
* Digital products
* Gift cards
* Perishable goods

Once a returned product passes quality inspection, the replacement or refund process begins.

Customers may initiate returns through the "My Orders" section of their account or by contacting customer support.

---

## 9. Do all products include a warranty?

Warranty coverage depends on the manufacturer and product category.

Products with warranty include warranty details on the product page and invoice.

Customers requesting warranty service should keep:

* Original invoice
* Product packaging (if available)
* Warranty documentation

Physical damage caused by misuse, accidental damage, or unauthorized repairs is generally not covered under warranty.

---

## 10. Do you offer discounts and promotional offers?

NovaCart regularly offers discounts during seasonal sales and special promotional events.

Membership benefits may include exclusive discount coupons.

Only one promotional coupon can usually be applied per order unless specifically stated otherwise.

Discounts cannot normally be combined with other promotional campaigns unless mentioned in the offer terms.

Customers are encouraged to subscribe to newsletters to receive updates about upcoming promotions.

---

## 11. How is my personal information protected?

NovaCart values customer privacy and protects personal information using industry-standard security measures.

Customer information is collected only to process orders, improve services, and provide customer support.

Personal information is never sold to third parties.

Payment information is processed through secure payment providers and is not stored on our systems.

Customers may request updates or corrections to their personal information by contacting customer support.

---

## 12. How can I track my order?

After an order is shipped, customers receive a tracking number by email and SMS.

The tracking number can be entered on the courier's tracking page or viewed within the "My Orders" section of the customer account.

Tracking information may take several hours to become available after shipment.

---

NovaCart accepts:

* Digital Wallets

Payment availability may vary depending on the customer's location.
